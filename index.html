What am I all about?

Hi, I'm Tanner! Welcome to my website. Thank you for taking the time to come here to get to know a little more about me. I am super excited to share with you some of the things that I love the most!

The first thing that I would want anyone to know about me would be how I am a believer and disciple of Jesus Christ. I know that He is the redeemer of the world. I know that He loves me and every single person on the Earth. I know that He suffered for our pains and afflicions, that He died for our sins, and then on the third day, He rose from the dead. He did all this so that we may return to live with Him and our Father in Heaven, and to become like they are one day.

I would like to invite you to watch this video about Him and all that He has done for you and what He can do for you now!


If you would like to learn more about Christ, I invite you to click on this link! Here, you will visit the website of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints!

Please feel to browse the rest of my webiste. You can go to the About Me to learn about who I am and what I love to do. Also, enjoy the Tableau page where you will see some cool interactive visualizations made with tableau!

Thank you for visiting my website!
