Calisthenics

See more about what calisthenics is here!

In this image, these are Calisthenics rings that are used for this type of training. They train for balance and majority of the muscle groups.

Calisthenics is a type of workout that primarily uses body weight to build the large muscle groups. This type of workout focuses on building the entire body as one strong unit, rather than focusing on building only one type of muscle group.

Before Calisthenics, I had always done weight training in a weight room because that is what we would do for strength training in football. When I got to college, I wasn't sure if I wanted to keep doing heavy weight training because I was worried it would hurt my body. So, one day I went on a run and began doing some HIIT workouts, when someone approached me and asked if I wanted to join the BYU calisthenics club and I told them I had never heard of it before and they told me to come to the club meeting to find out. Ever since then I have been so pleased with how it trains my body and helps me feel good!





Football

As I grew up in a small town in Texas, football became my life! It was such an amazing experience. I was able to start playing tackle football when I was 10 years old and stopped when I was 18 as a senior! This sport taught me so much about life. I learned about hard work, dedication, friendship, and trust. I loved every moment of it!

I started out playing as a tight end when I was 10, and then I moved to be a running back on offense and a strong safety on defense. I absolutely loved both! From there, when I was in middle school, I stayed as a running back but then was converted to be a free safety on defense. I loved every second of it! Finally as a Junior on Varsity, I was placed to be a cornerback until the end of my senior year! It was such a great experience to be out there playing with my friends and learning from my coaches.





Running
